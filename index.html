About Me

Tung Yu-Chih is a CS PhD candidate at University of Michigan-Ann Arbor, advised by Prof. Kang G. Shin. His research intrest is building mobile applications or security systems based on physical-layer signal processing. Yu-Chih received M.S. from National Taiwan University at 2012. He used to work as a research assistant (intern) at HP Labs with the Mobility team, CSAIL MIT with Prof. Dina Katabi, and CITI Academia Sinica with Dr. Kate Ching-Ju Lin. His current work has been published at top-ranked conferences such as ACM MobiCom or ACM CCS and under the process of being commercialized.

Selected Projects

EchoTag09/2015

EchoTag enables commodity phones to tag and remember indoor locations without requiring any additional sensor or pre-installed infrastructure. The main idea behind EchoTag is to actively generate acoustic signatures by transmitting a inaudible sound signal with a phone’s speakers and sensing its reflections with the phone’s microphones.

My Role: Implementing the system at Android by integrating audio signal processing and Java/JNI/Matlab/LibSVM

CSISec05/2014

A novel security system that ensures no leakage of channel state information (CSI) in wireless networks. CSISec utiliies the similar technology of zero-knowledge proof and signal cancellation to secure the transmission without modifying cleint devices (such as mobile phones).

My Role: Discovering this potential vunlearbility and implementing the system in WARP embedded testbeds

TurboRate09/2012

A rate adaptation protocol solves the packet collision issue in Multi-user MIMO networks. Implementation and evaluation validate 1.7x and 2.3x throughput gains for 2-antenna and 3-antenna AP scenario respectively. This project is nomiated as one of three best paper candidates in ACM MobiCom 2012.

My Role: Implementing and testing the proposed wireless protocols in USRP embeded testbeds

Labor Rights App10/2011

Kingfong (Labor Law Console) is a mobile application written for all employees and employers in Taiwan/China to know their rights and calculate what they should get, such as pension and overwork payment. It also provides console function to post questions which will be replied by Kingfong cooperation.

My Role: Designing the system model and user interface in Java and Objective-C
